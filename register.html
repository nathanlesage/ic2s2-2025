Visa Information

Citizens of non-Schengen countries may need to apply for a visa to participate in IC2S2 2025. Please consult Migrationsverket's website to identify whether you need a visa to enter Sweden, and, if so, how to apply.

You will automatically receive a letter of invitation upon successful registration which you can provide when applying for a visa. Should you require additional documents or details from the conference organizers in order to apply for a visa, please send an email to {{ site.emails.visa }}
