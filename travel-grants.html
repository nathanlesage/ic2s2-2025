Travel Grants

We are proud to announce that a number of travel grants are available to support attendees with limited travel resources.

Aim of Grants / Eligibility

The grants aim to especially support attendees with limited travel resources and attendees from countries where computational social science is not yet well established.

Procedure

To apply for the travel grant, please fill in this travel grant application form. You will be asked for some basic information, your abstract ID, a short statement as to why you would like to attend {{ site.title }} and how attendance will benefit your education/career, as well as a short statement describing your lack of financial support, and justifying your needs for the travel grant.

The deadline for travel grant application submissions is {{ site.data.travel_grants.application_deadline }}.

Notifications will be sent out on {{ site.data.travel_grants.grant_notification }}.

Note that there may be more meritorious applications than we will be able to award. In all cases, the grants and/or awards should be fully spent on the conference. Grants are awarded to attendants only upon providing receipts of expenses made in light of attending the conference. This is done in the month after the conference itself, and no deposits can be made beforehand.
